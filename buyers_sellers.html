Sellers' Insights

The Premium Predicted chart illustrates a clear long-term upward trend in market premiums. After a period of fluctuation, premiums accelerate sharply around 2022 and are projected to remain high through the coming decade. This suggests sustained demand and cost pressures driving prices up. The strong forecast beyond 2028 points to a structural shift where elevated demand and limited supply keep premiums resilient.

The Quota Predicted chart shows cyclical movements in quota availability—peaking around 2018, dipping afterward, and recovering in the forecast horizon. These swings indicate supply is heavily shaped by policy or allocation decisions. The expected increase in quotas after 2025 could help balance demand pressure and stabilize premiums if the expansion is sustained.

The Quota vs Premium view highlights a strong inverse relationship: as quotas rise, premiums tend to fall. Scarce quotas intensify competition and push prices up, while expanded supply eases pricing pressure. This pattern underscores how policy timing and supply management directly shape market outcomes.
