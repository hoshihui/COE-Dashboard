Buyers/Sellers Dashboard

  1. Home
  2. Buyers/Sellers Dashboard

Whether you’re buying your first car or upgrading to a new one, timing is everything. Our tool helps everyday buyers track COE price trends and find the best moment to buy, so you get the most value. For sellers, we show the ideal time to sell—whether on your own or via a dealer—helping you maximize returns and make your next move stress-free.

Dashboard Insights

  • Most buyers prefer affordable categories, with Category A dominating purchases.
  • Monthly purchase trends peak in December and May, likely due to promotions.
  • Top buyer segments are users aged 25–35 with high engagement rates.

  • Most sellers aim to sell their cars within 3–6 months of purchase.
  • Peak selling months align with buyers’ high-demand periods: December and May.
  • Sellers using dealers often achieve faster sales but at slightly lower margins.

  • COE prices show seasonal fluctuations with historical lows in February and August.
  • Category B and D tend to have the most volatile price changes.
  • Monitoring trends can help buyers and sellers optimize timing for transactions.

  • Buyers respond well to promotions and discounts, driving spikes in activity.
  • Sellers’ preferred sales channels affect time-to-sale and average sale price.
  • Understanding both buyer and seller behavior helps predict market trends.

Dashboard Insights

  • The market is highly competitive, with small price differences driving buyer decisions.
  • Government policies are influencing category affordability trends.
  • Market growth is concentrated in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities, with strong online adoption.
